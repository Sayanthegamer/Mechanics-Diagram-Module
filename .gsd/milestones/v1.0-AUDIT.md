## Concerns
- **Euler Solver Drifts**: In systems with high friction/damping or complex spring oscillations, basic Euler integration drifts over time.
- **Isometric 3D Math Projection Ambiguity**: Projection mappings can have multiple 3D coordinate combinations mapping to the same 2D screen coordinate. Resolving mouse position assuming $Z=0$ solves dragging on the plane, but could be enhanced for general cases if needed.

## Recommendations
1. Propose implementing a Runge-Kutta 4th Order (RK4) integration option in the future to resolve Euler integration numerical energy drifts.
2. Consider adding visual guidelines showing drag planes when dragging vectors in isometric 3D space to assist the user.
